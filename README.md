**Project: Annual Sales Report Dashboard (Excel)**

**Project Overview**

* Developed an interactive Annual Sales Report Dashboard in Microsoft Excel to analyze 2022 sales performance.
* Cleaned and analyzed sales data using Pivot Tables, Pivot Charts, Slicers, and Excel formulas.
* Created a dashboard to answer key business questions and generate actionable insights.

**Key Analysis**

* Compared monthly sales and order trends.
* Identified the highest sales and order month.
* Analyzed customer purchases by gender.
* Examined order status distribution.
* Identified the top 10 revenue-contributing states.
* Analyzed sales by age group and gender.
* Evaluated sales contribution by different sales channels.
* Determined the highest-selling product category.

**Key Insights**

* Women contributed around **65%** of total purchases.
* **Maharashtra, Karnataka, and Uttar Pradesh** were the top-performing states.
* Customers aged **30–49 years** generated the highest sales.
* **Amazon, Flipkart, and Myntra** were the leading sales channels.
* Recommended targeting women aged **30–49** in the top-performing states through offers and promotions on major e-commerce platforms.
